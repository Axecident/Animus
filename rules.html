Rules

Here are the rules of Animus. Please follow them.

1. No abusing other members or any staff. Abuse-like jokes (banter) are allowed as long as the person you are directing them towards knows for certain it is a joke!

2. No being racist to anyone. Racist jokes can sometimes be allowed but only in voice channels. Where there are people you know will not get offended by them.

3. No exploiting any potentially rule breaking and/or annoying features of bots in Animus.

4. No ecchi and/or pornographic material of any kind is allowed except for in the NSFW channel.

5. No porn/ecchi material of girls/boys that look like little kids (despite what their age or boob/dick size is).

6. No disturbing or non pornographic/ecchi material is allowed in the NSFW channel.

7. No false reporting of someone breaking the rules.

8. No messaging developers for help with your problems. If you want to report a problem with the website or something else coding related. Please let a staff member (Moderator, Admin, Super Admin) know instead.

9. If you need help do not go to the highest staff member right away. Go to the lowest first (Moderator). If you cannot get in contact with them then go to an Admin and so on.

10. No spamming is allowed in any channel of Animus.

11. No "talking" to bots is allowed except for in the bot-talk channel.

12. No harassment sexual or otherwise is tolerated on Animus.

If you see rule(s) being broken. Please alert moderators to the chat immediately by typing "@Moderator Rule(s) broken"
